Karaoke Box

My entry for Algorithm Arena Weekly Challenge #17: Karaoke Box.

Note: For now, only the song "Karma" by Taylor Swift is supported, as Karaoke Box requires songtexts with precise syllable-timing, which have to be created manually. Since I legally do not have the right to directly include the song here, please open the song on YouTube (must be that exact video!) or Spotify and press play and the start button simultaneously (e.g. by playing the song on your phone while having Karaoke Box opened on a computer).
